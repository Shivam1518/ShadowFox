Team Achievements

  • RCB has reached the IPL finals three times (2009, 2011, 2020).
  • RCB holds the record for the highest team score in IPL history (263/5 in 2015).
  • The team has produced some of the finest cricketers, including Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle.
  • RCB is one of the most popular IPL teams with a massive fan base worldwide.
